Innovation

We actively shape innovation

For many years, we have been actively working with our partners from industry, science, society and politics on the coordinated design of innovation processes.

In this way, we help to create the conditions for your innovations to be successfully and sustainably brought to market and to have a positive impact on society.

Innovation from all sides

In this role, we support federal, state and European Union project funding agencies by advancing the digitization of project funding.

On the other hand, as a research-based medium-sized company, we are very familiar with the innovation hurdles and practical challenges in innovation processes.

We take your innovations to their goal

With this particular perspective on innovation, we can help you identify and address bottlenecks in the innovation pipeline.

We connect you with the right players from our network so that your innovations succeed.
