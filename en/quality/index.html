Quality

Our goal: your satisfaction

The satisfaction of our customers is a fundamental goal for us. We consider our commitment to the exceptional quality of our products, services and processes to be the most important prerequisite for this.

For this reason, we firmly established a certified quality management system in our company as early as 1996.

One company, two quality management systems

In order to meet the special requirements of our broad field of activities, we are now practicing two separate quality management systems in accordance with the international standards DIN EN ISO 9001 and DIN EN ISO 13485. Our quality processes are subject to constant monitoring.

Quality is corporate culture for us

For us, quality management does not mean overhead, but rather setting the highest standards for our own performance and anchoring quality centrally in our corporate culture. In addition to the design, development and support of information and communication systems, since 2014 our quality management also covers the design, manufacture and service of medical devices.

Key aspects of our quality management

  • Customer orientation
  • Employee satisfaction
  • Positive error culture and continuous improvement
  • Long-term and cooperative supplier relationships
  • Risik Management (DIN EN ISO 14971)
  • Software Lifecycle (DIN EN 62304)
  • Usabilty Engineering (DIN EN 62366)
