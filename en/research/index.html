Research

An interdisciplinary approach

In cooperation with our partners, we conduct research in interdisciplinary projects to develop building blocks for the future of metrology, medicine, mobility and industry, and thus actively contribute to cross-industry technology transfer. For us, sustainability in technology development as well as fair cooperation and exploitation models are of central importance.

Responsible research and development

We define responsible research and development holistically: business, technology and ethics must not be at odds with each other. This is the only way to ensure that research results can also be successfully transferred into practice.

From medicine to aerospace

Our research focuses are as diverse as we ourselves are. Our research topics include:

  • Medical Device Technology
  • Industry 4.0
  • Automotive
  • Aerospace
